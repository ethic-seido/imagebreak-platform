# ImageBreak Platform

Too many tools, not enough time. ImageBreak brings images, video and music creation into one place — with or without AI. Built in France 🇫🇷

**ImageBreak** is a next-generation AI-powered platform for image, video, audio, and full digital artwork generation.

Built and operated by **Ethic Seido**, ImageBreak combines multiple AI creative technologies into a unified platform for generating and transforming digital content.

👉 Official platform: https://imagebreak.com  

---

## 🌐 What is ImageBreak?

ImageBreak is a multi-modal AI creation platform that enables users to generate and transform digital content across multiple formats:

- Images
- Videos
- Music
- Full digital artworks

The platform is designed for complete AI-powered creative production workflows.

---

## ⚙️ Core Features

### 🖼️ AI Image Generation & Enhancement
- Generate images from text prompts
- Enhance image quality and resolution
- Restore low-quality or damaged visuals
- Remove backgrounds and isolate objects
- Optimize visuals for digital and web usage

---

### 🎬 AI Video Generation
- Generate videos from text prompts
- Transform images into dynamic video sequences
- Create visual content for marketing and social media
- AI-powered storytelling and motion generation

---

### 🎵 AI Music Generation
- Generate music from text prompts
- Create audio tracks for multimedia content
- Produce cinematic, ambient, and creative soundscapes
- Support AI-assisted music composition workflows

---

### 🎨 AI Full Artwork Generation
- Generate complete digital artworks from text prompts
- Create finished artistic pieces across multiple styles
- Produce illustrations, paintings, abstract works, cinematic art, and 3D-inspired visuals
- Support professional and personal creative production
